About ADHD 💜

ADHD is not a lack of intelligence or effort. It is a difference in executive functioning. Many people with ADHD are highly creative, intuitive, empathetic, and capable of deep focus when genuinely interested.

Struggling to start tasks is not laziness. It is often difficulty with task initiation — a neurological barrier, not a character flaw.

Systems designed with compassion make a difference. This tool is one small example of that.
